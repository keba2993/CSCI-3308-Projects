How to play: Tetris

Objective: The goal of the game is rotate and move differnt block shapes to aline them in rows at the bottom of the game board. Be careful! Make sure you fill up some rows before the blacks stack to the top!

Tetris Official Rules

=========================================================================================

How to play: Tetris

Controls: This game is run by four simple controls:
A: move the block piece left
D: move the block piece right
S: progress block piece downwards
W: rotate block piece to the right

How to play: Tetris

Scoring Points: Points are scored by completed horizontal rows within the game board. You will receive 10 points for every row completed. Blocks above completed rows will fall, don't let the blocks exceed the height limit!

How to Play: 2048

Objective: It's quite simply really! Your goal is to sum up blocks until you produce a 2048 block! Watch out though, only blocks with the same number can be combined!

=========================================================================================

How to play: 2048

Controls: The controls for this game are also quite simple:
A: slide all tiles left
D: slide all tiles right
S: slide all tiles down
W: slide all tiles up

How to play: 2048

Scoring Points: Points are scored by combining like numbered tiles! Each merge of tiles adds to your score and gets you closer to the 2048 tile! Be quick but be strategic. It's not as easy as it sounds!

=========================================================================================

Playing them Together

The real challenge here is playing the games at the same time!! Tetris and 2048 are simple but challenging puzzles on their own, however, it takes a higher level of brain power to successfully operate both games simultaneaously! Do you have what it takes?

Playing them Together

The controls are the same as previously stated for each game, but just keep in mind that each key presss will affect both games! Keep track of where your blocks are falling in Tetris, but be aware, you may move the 2048 tiles in the wrong direction!
